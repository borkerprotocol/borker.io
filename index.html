What is Borker?

Originally launched on top of Dogecoin, Borker is a layer 1 protocol for many utxo-based blockchains, including Bitcoin. It enables a Twitter-like microblogging experience as a global public utility.

Borker is open, decentralized, borderless, permissionless, pseudonymous, immutable, and censorship-resistant.

Open

Borker is open to everyone to build upon and use.

Immutable

Borker is exactly as immutable as whatever blockchain it rides on. By design, users can filter and delete borks from the Borker Network. But the blockchain never forgets, so be careful what you say.

Censorship Resistant

Publishing and consuming content on the Borker Network is equivelent to sending and receiving transactions on the Bitcoin or Dogecoin p2p networks. And once a transaction is sent, it cannot be unsent.
