What is Borker?

Borker Protocol is an application layer protocol designed to ride on top of the Bitcoin p2p network and similar UTXO-based, transparent blockchains. It relies on the openess, security, and immutability of the underlying networks.

Borker is open, decentralized, borderless, permissionless, pseudonymous, immutable, and censorship-resistant.

Open

Borker is open to everyone to build upon and use. There are no gatekeepers. There is no way to prevent someone from participating.

Immutable

Borker is exactly as immutable its underlying blockchains. By design, users can filter and delete borks from the Borker Network. But the blockchain never forgets, so be careful what you say.

Censorship Resistant

Publishing and consuming content on the Borker Network is equivelent to sending and receiving transactions on the underlying blockchain network. Once a transaction is sent, it cannot be unsent.
